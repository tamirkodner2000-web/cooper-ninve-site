# Internal Linking Plan - Cooper Ninve

## Goal
Define the internal linking structure for Cooper Ninve’s new website.

Internal links should help:
- Users navigate between relevant pages
- Google understand the site structure
- Product pages support each other
- Landing pages connect to relevant authority pages
- Agents and business clients reach the correct conversion flow

# Internal Linking Rules

- Use natural Hebrew anchor text.
- Do not use generic links like “click here”.
- Every important page should be reachable within 2 clicks from the homepage.
- Every product page should link back to Insurance Solutions.
- Every product page should include a contact CTA.
- Every audience page should link to relevant product pages.
- Landing pages should minimize navigation distractions.
- Blog articles should support product pages with contextual links.
